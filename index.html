RendezYOU
RendezYou is a web app that tucks itself neatly in the travel and tourism sector, connecting travellers with 'out of the box' tours given by locals. Users log on with the freedom to create tours as a TOURGUIDE; or kick back as a TOURIST and browse through our database of tours - created by users, for the users.

RendezYou was developed with two other web developers as a final project at BitMaker Labs.

Built on: Ruby, Rails, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, PostGres, SQL, AJAX, Google Maps.
